Biological Disasters
What is Biological Disasters ?

Biological Disaster is an event or series of events caused by the spread of infectious diseases that affect human populations, animals, or plants. Biological disasters can occur naturally, as with epidemics or pandemics , or as a result of deliberate acts of bio-terrorism.

Types of Biological Disasters


Zoonotic diseases are infections that are transmitted from animals to humans, such as Ebola, SARS, and COVID-19.

Human Caused Biological Disasters


An epidemic is an outbreak of an infectious disease that affects a large number of people in a specific area, while a pandemic is a global epidemic that spreads across multiple continents or worldwide.
